Prosciutto Salmon with Garlic Butter

A simple yet elegant dish with crispy prosciutto and rich garlic butter, perfect for a healthy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 4 slices of prosciutto
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Wrap each salmon fillet with a slice of prosciutto, tucking the ends underneath.
  4. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Place the salmon fillets prosciutto-side down and sear for 2-3 minutes until the prosciutto is crispy.
  5. Flip the salmon and add butter and minced garlic to the skillet. Spoon the garlic butter over the salmon as it melts, cooking for another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 5-7 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked to your desired doneness (internal temperature of 145°F/63°C for medium).
  7. Remove from the oven, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve with lemon wedges on the side.
